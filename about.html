About Me

My name is Justice Jones and I am a Senior at the University of Iowa in the Tippie College of Business. I am working toward a bachelor's degree in Business Analytics and Marketing. I am from Kalona, Iowa and previously attended Mid-Prairie High School. Outside of class, I enjoy college football in the fall—tailgating and watching games with friends is one of my favorite activities. I am a lifelong Iowa Hawkeyes and Green Bay Packers fan.

I also spend a lot of time outdoors. Fishing with friends during the summer is my perfect day, and I enjoy working out and playing basketball. Since sophomore year, I've been part of an intramural basketball team that competes each spring. I also try to make a trip to Wisconsin each year to fish the Great Lakes with my grandpa. Looking ahead, I'm excited to graduate and start the next chapter in my career.
