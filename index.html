Welcome

Hi! I'm Justice. I am student at the University of Iowa majoring in Business Analytics and Marketing and this is my website for BAIS:3300.

During my time at the University of Iowa, I've balanced academics with a variety of professional roles that have given me experience in business, operations, and hands-on work. Most recently, I worked as a Business Development and Administrative Assistant at ProLift Garage Doors, where I supported sales efforts by preparing proposals, attending networking events, and coordinating schedules. I also served as an Operations Intern at Houses Into Homes, where I created an efficient warehouse map, managed inventory of donated items, and supported furniture deliveries to families in need. These roles have strengthened my organizational, communication, and problem-solving skills.

Alongside these experiences, I've worked extensively in the construction industry. As a laborer with Chris Schlabach Construction and BHE Concrete, I collaborated on large-scale residential and commercial projects, reading blueprints, surveying sites, pouring concrete, and framing steel buildings. I enjoy this type of work because I like to do things with my hands and see the direct results of my effort—whether it's a driveway poured, a building framed, or a project completed with my team. These jobs taught me the value of precision, teamwork, and the satisfaction of building something tangible.

I also have experience in event operations as a Follow Spot Operator with Sound Concepts, where I’ve worked over 40 Iowa sporting events, including NCAA tournament games, ensuring precise lighting and smooth event execution. These diverse roles have given me a balance of technical, analytical, and hands-on skills that I believe will serve me well in my career.
